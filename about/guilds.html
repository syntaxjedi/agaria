Guild Description Attributes Requirements
Warrior The warrior is a brute force fighter. They are excelent at distracting the enemy and have a high armor rating and absorb a lot of damage. +10% armor rating when wearing full tempered plate armor none
Assassin The assassin lurks in the shadows and take out their targets without being seen. The assassin uses small daggers for speed and poisons for effect. +20% crit chance when attacking out of stealth none
Archer The archer is skilled in long ranged damage. Always looking for a vantage point, the archer is very stealthy and good at covering his tracks. +20% crit chance when focused none
Spell Caster Spell casters harness the very energy of the universe to seemingly bend reality. They use all four elements to their advantage: fire, water, earth, and air. +10% resillience when health and mana below 10% none
Paladin The most important things to paladins are their sword skills and their faith. Paladins have the ability to call down miracles and cleanse their allies.
